About Me

I am a machine learning researcher at HSBC. I completed my PhD at the Newcastle University and KU Leuven supervised by Prof. Sara Walker (University of Birmingham), Dr. Stephen McGough (Newcastle University) and Dr. Hussain Kazmi (KU Leuven University). I was also a postgraduate research assistant at the Alan Turing Institute. My research focuses on applying machine learning techniques to energy analytics. Beyond that, I am interested in developing responsible and ethical AI products that achieve product-market fit. I hold a Masters in Big Data from University of Stirling and a Bachelors degree in Information and Communication Technology.

Research Interests

  • Data science: machine learning, deep learning,transfer learning, classification, regression
  • Forecasting: time-series forecasting, time-series analysis
